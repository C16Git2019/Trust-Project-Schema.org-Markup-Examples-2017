Keanu Reeves puts the play back in gunplay: Stargazing

By Garnet FraserToronto Star
Sun., May 27, 2018

In the first John Wick movie, characters said Wick’s full names 11 times; in the sequel, a mere eight. Please let John Wick 3’s dialogue be nothing but gifted, slumming British actors saying “John Wick” for 110 minutes and squeezing fresh meaning out of it every time.

Failing that, maybe in this one somebody kills John Wick’s ... uncle? Plants? Party? Elephant?
